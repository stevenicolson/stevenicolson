Biography

I was born in West Croydon, south London in the late1960,s.Played football for Crystal Palace youth, county tennis and is a 50 break man at snooker. After completing Alevels I worked in insurance for 3 years, all the while performing at the Croydon Youth Theatre. When I felt ready I went to drama school in London- The Webber Douglas Academy of Dramatic Art. Left in 1990 so I am celebrating 30 years in the business!
